SCOUTING

In a typical 12 months, we'll do:

Climbing, Rafting, Archery, Sailing, Hiking, Camping, Cooking, Craft Work and lots, lots more...

Like the sound of it? Aged between six and 14? Come and join in with the activities we run in the heart of Upminster.

Here at 2nd Upminster Scout Group we have- Beavers, Cubs and Scouts. Whichever one you fit into you can be sure you'll have fun, and you're sure to have a go at things you've never tried before, and will hopefully learn some new skills.
