CHURCH PARADES

We are a church sponsored group and our members are required to attend the following church parades and special service:

Parade 2024
Lent Sunday 3rd March, 9:30am
- Sunday 16th June, 9:30am
Harvest Thanksgiving Sunday 29th September, 9:30am
