UNIFORM

You don’t need to buy any uniform for when your child first starts attending suitable casual clothes and footwear will be fine.  Once they’ve decided that Scouting is for them, they’ll need to wear the official uniform so they can be formally invested into the group.  The leaders will tell you which items of uniform are required for their section and where you can get it.  We try to recycle second hand uniform where possible; if this interests you please speak to your section leader.
