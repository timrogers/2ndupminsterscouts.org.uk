WELCOME

Welcome to the website of the 2nd Upminster Scout Group. We are a church-affiliated Scout Group based at St Laurence Church in Upminster, Essex and are one of 18 Scout Groups belonging to Hornchurch District Scout Association. Our Scout Group has three sections - Beavers, Cubs and Scouts â€“ which together provide weekly Scouting activities for young people between the ages of six and fourteen.

The Scouting formula is simple: using volunteer adult leaders and basic facilities, Scouting helps young people from all backgrounds to develop universally valued qualities of trust, empathy, confidence, integrity and a spirit of adventure. Life skills are learned to help young people become tomorrow's happiest, healthiest and most productive citizens, some of whom will be tomorrow's leaders.
