BEAVERS

Beaver Scouts belong to the youngest section of the Scouting family. Their activities are based around making things, outdoor activities, playing games, going out on visits, investigating nature, listening to stories, learning how to be safe and most importantly, making new friends.

 

The Beaver Scout Promise


I promise to do my best
To be kind and helpful
And to love God.


The Beaver Scout Motto

Be prepared.
